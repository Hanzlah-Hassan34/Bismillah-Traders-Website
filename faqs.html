Application & Dosage

How to calculate pesticide dosage? ‚ñº

Always follow the manufacturer's label instructions carefully. For example, Actara 25WG should be used at 60 grams per acre in 100-120 liters of water. Dosage may vary based on crop type, pest severity, and environmental conditions.

Example Calculation:

  • Product: Actara 25WG
  • Dosage: 60g per acre
  • Water: 100-120 liters per acre
  • Coverage: Apply during early morning or evening
What is the best time to apply pesticides? ‚ñº

The ideal time for pesticide application is during early morning (6-9 AM) or late evening (5-7 PM) when temperatures are cooler and wind speeds are low. Avoid application during midday heat, windy conditions, or before expected rainfall.

Can I mix different pesticides together? ‚ñº

Some pesticides can be tank-mixed, but always check compatibility first. Never mix products without consulting the manufacturer's guidelines or our experts. Incompatible mixing can reduce effectiveness or cause crop damage.

Safety & Precautions

What safety equipment should I use while spraying? ‚ñº

Always wear protective equipment including masks, gloves, long-sleeved clothing, and eye protection. Ensure proper ventilation and avoid eating, drinking, or smoking during application. Wash thoroughly after use.

Safety Checklist:

  • ‚úì Protective mask or respirator
  • ‚úì Chemical-resistant gloves
  • ‚úì Long-sleeved shirt and pants
  • ‚úì Eye protection
  • ‚úì Closed shoes
  • ‚úì Proper ventilation
How should I store pesticides safely? ‚ñº

Store pesticides in original containers in a cool, dry, well-ventilated area away from children, pets, food, and feed. Keep them locked and maintain proper temperature. Never transfer to food or drink containers.
